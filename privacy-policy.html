Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Music Hub is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Local Storage Data: Your music preferences, playlists, and settings are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage.
  • Search Queries: When you search for music, queries are sent to YouTube API but are not stored on our servers.
  • Anonymous Usage Data: We may collect anonymous analytics to improve the app (feature usage, error reports).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data only to:

  • Provide music streaming functionality
  • Remember your preferences across sessions
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Debug and fix technical issues

3. Data Storage & Security

All user data is stored locally on your device. No personal information is transmitted to or stored on our servers. Your data remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to sync across devices (future feature).

4. Third-Party Services

Music Hub integrates with:

  • YouTube Data API v3: For music search and streaming. YouTube's privacy policy applies to their services.
  • Vercel: For hosting and API functions. Vercel may collect standard server logs.
  • Font Awesome & Google Fonts: For icons and typography.

5. Children's Privacy

Music Hub is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all local data via browser settings
  • Disable certain features (like analytics)
  • Stop using the app at any time

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
